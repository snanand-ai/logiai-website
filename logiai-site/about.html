The core insight

Logistics is not a workflow problem. It is an optimization problem. Every shipment represents a complex multi-variable decision — price vs win probability, margin vs service level, speed vs cost, risk vs reliability. These decisions happen millions of times a year, are never modelled systematically, and simply vanish into operational history.
