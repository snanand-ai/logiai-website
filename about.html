The Founder

Sachin Anand

Founder & CEO, LogiAI | Singapore

I spent 20+ years inside global logistics at some of the world's most respected freight forwarders. I built businesses, managed teams across Asia, and closed hundreds of millions in freight deals.

And every single day, I watched the same thing happen: critical intelligence — buyer behavior, carrier patterns, margin signals, escalation triggers — generated by every email, every quote, every exception — simply vanish.

No system captured it. No team analyzed it. It disappeared into operational history, recreated from scratch every single time. I realized: logistics isn't a workflow problem. It's an optimization problem. And nobody was solving it.

That's why I founded LogiAI. Not to build another TMS or ERP plugin. To build the intelligence layer that global trade has always needed but never had.
